Welcome!

Thank you so much for visiting our website! All of our bakery items are made with gluten-free ingredients. We know and understand how difficult it can be to find not only sweets that are gluten-free, but ones that are made safe for consumption due to cross-contamination. This specialized bakery allows us to cater to those with even the most extreme forms of this allergy. We also offer desserts that are dairy-free and nut-free as well since dairy and nut ingredients are prevalent in dessert choices. Please check out our Bakery Items page at the top of the screen. If you would like to know more about and/or register to learn of our newest/latest products, please see our About Us page at the top of your screen.

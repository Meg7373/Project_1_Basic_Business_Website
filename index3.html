About Us

We are a dedicated gluten-free bakery that offers bakery items that are safe for even the most severe gluten allergies as well as dairy- and nut-free options as well. In a world where gluten is every-present in the majority of restaurant businesses, it is hard for those with these allergies to go and eat safely. Our goal here is to provide that as well as products that are not only safe, but taste better than regular pastries. We hope that you will swing by and come visit us!
